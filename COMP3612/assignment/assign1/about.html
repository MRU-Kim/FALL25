Our Story

COMP 3612 Assign 1 began with a simple belief: that every programmer deserves clothes that not only look exceptional but feel extraordinary. Founded in 2025 by Kim Carino, our company emerged from a shared frustration with the compromise between quality and affordability in the coding attire marker, as well as a strong desire to get an A grade in COMP2612.
